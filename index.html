Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download YouTube thumbnails?

Simply paste the YouTube video URL(s) in the input field above and click 'Get Thumbnails'. Then select your preferred quality and click the download button. For multiple URLs, click a quality button to download all thumbnails as a ZIP file.

What thumbnail qualities are available?

We support multiple resolutions including 4K (3840x2160), 1080p (1920x1080), 720p (1280x720), and 480p (854x480).

How does bulk download work?

Enter multiple YouTube URLs (one per line) in the input field, click 'Get Thumbnails', select a quality from the dropdown, and click the download button for that quality to download all thumbnails in a single ZIP file.

Is this service free to use?

Absolutely! YT Thumb Grabber is completely free with no hidden charges or premium tiers.

Do you store my downloaded thumbnails?

No, we don't store any of your downloaded thumbnails. All processing happens in your browser.

Can I use this on mobile devices?

Yes, our website is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.
